Plate VII

The gardener

Constantinus chirila · perennial, hardy · native to Romania, naturalised in Britain

The author, observed from life

I'm Constantin, a Romanian-born British software architect and full-stack engineer. I started on the design side of the craft over a decade ago, crossed into engineering, and have been building products for clients since 2013: first the interfaces, then the systems behind them, and now the whole thing end to end.

These days I work with founders and product teams who need one accountable pair of hands across architecture, back-end and front-end: the hard decisions made visible, the software shipped, and a codebase left in a state your team can inherit without me in the room.
